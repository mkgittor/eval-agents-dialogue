# Approved Sources

## Tier 1 — Primary & Wire Services
Direct, original reporting. Prefer these above all others.

| Outlet | Domain | Coverage Focus |
|---|---|---|
| Reuters | reuters.com | General news, markets, geopolitics |
| Bloomberg | bloomberg.com | Finance, markets, business |
| Associated Press (AP) | apnews.com | General news, geopolitics |

## Tier 2 — Financial & Business Press
High editorial standards; may carry opinion and analysis alongside reporting.

| Outlet | Domain | Coverage Focus |
|---|---|---|
| Financial Times | ft.com | Global finance, economics, business |
| The Wall Street Journal | wsj.com | US markets, business, finance |
| The Economist | economist.com | Economics, geopolitics, long-form analysis |
| Barron's | barrons.com | Investing, markets |
| Nikkei Asia | asia.nikkei.com | Asia-Pacific business and markets |

## Tier 3 — National Newspapers of Record
Strong general editorial standards; broader scope than financial press.

| Outlet | Domain | Coverage Focus |
|---|---|---|
| The New York Times | nytimes.com | General, business, geopolitics |
| The Washington Post | washingtonpost.com | General, policy, geopolitics |
| The Guardian | theguardian.com | General, international |
| Le Monde | lemonde.fr | French and international news |
| Frankfurter Allgemeine | faz.net | German and European affairs |

## Tier 4 — Official & Primary Sources
Not press outlets — use for primary documents and data only.

| Source Type | Examples |
|---|---|
| Regulatory filings | SEC EDGAR, Companies House, SEDAR+ |
| Central banks | Federal Reserve, ECB, Bank of England |
| Government databases | Official Gazette, legislative records |
| Company IR pages | Investor relations sections of official corporate websites |
| International bodies | IMF, World Bank, WTO, BIS |

---

## Usage Notes
- **Tier 1** sources take precedence when the same story is covered across tiers.
- **Tier 4** sources are authoritative for facts but do not replace editorial reporting for context and analysis.
- For outlets not listed here, verify editorial standards and ownership before use. When in doubt, exclude.
- This list covers general-purpose research. Append a **Domain-Specific Sources** section below for specialised coverage areas (e.g., pharma, energy, legal).
